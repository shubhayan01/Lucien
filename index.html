Why Lucien Academy

We don't just teach — we cultivate confident leaders

Founded with a vision to go beyond traditional learning, Lucien Academy provides a nurturing environment where children are encouraged to think critically, speak confidently, and create fearlessly.

Experienced Educators
All teachers are guided through the Universal Design for Learning framework with methods to ensure equal opportunity for every student.
Performance & Showcase Opportunities
Students participate in real showcases, mock conferences, debates, and storytelling events — building genuine real-world confidence.
Safe, Inclusive & Inspiring
We emphasize Culturally Responsive Pedagogy and create a welcoming environment where every child feels seen, heard, and valued.
Open to Parents
Parents are welcome to observe and attend class, giving students the opportunity to speak publicly in front of adults — just like the real world.
Global Citizens
We help students develop cultural awareness, critical thinking, and the characteristics to become actively engaged global citizens.

What ages and grades does Lucien Academy serve?
Lucien Academy offers programs for students from Kindergarten all the way through Grade 12. Classes are designed for primary, junior, intermediate, and high school students, with curriculum tailored to each stage of development.
What programs does Lucien Academy offer?
Our core programs include Public Speaking, Creative Writing, Drama & Performing Arts, French & Bilingual Communication, and Communication & Leadership Skills. We also offer expert-led workshops bookable at your school or organization.
Do you offer French or bilingual instruction?
Yes! Lucien Academy proudly offers bilingual instruction in English and/or French. We offer French conversation, French public speaking, French creative writing, and bilingual presentation skills — ideal for students in French Immersion or those expanding their language skills.
Can parents observe or attend classes?
Absolutely. We create an atmosphere where parents are welcome to observe and attend class as they prefer. This actually benefits students — it gives them the opportunity to practice speaking publicly in front of adults, just as they would in the real world.
What is the UDL approach used by educators?
Our educators are guided through the Universal Design for Learning (UDL) framework. This gives them the methods and tools to ensure all students have equal opportunity to learn, with the ability to customize lessons to meet individual students' unique needs and learning styles.
Can Lucien Academy workshops come to our school?
Yes! In addition to our in-house programs, Lucien Academy offers expert-led workshops delivered directly at your school, daycare, or community organization — from performing arts and public speaking to mental health, physiotherapy, and career exploration.
Do students get to publish their own work?
Yes! One of the highlights of our Creative Writing program is that we provide students with the opportunity to publish their own story books. It's a tangible, meaningful achievement that students and their families treasure.
Are there free workshops included in the programs?
Yes! We offer our enrolled students the opportunity to attend free workshops conducted by professionals from other fields of study — helping increase students' knowledge across a wide variety of subjects beyond our core curriculum.
