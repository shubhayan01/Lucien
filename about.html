Who We Are

Helping children find their voice

At Lucien Academy, we are dedicated to helping children build confidence and express themselves with clarity and purpose — because strong communication is the foundation of lifelong success.

At Lucien Academy, we are dedicated to helping children find their voice, build confidence, and express themselves with clarity and purpose. We believe that strong communication skills are the foundation of lifelong success — both academically and personally.

Founded with a vision to go beyond traditional learning, Lucien Academy provides a nurturing and inspiring environment where children are encouraged to think critically, speak confidently, and create fearlessly. Our programs are thoughtfully designed to develop not just skills, but self-belief.

We recognize that every child is unique. That's why we focus on personalized attention, small group learning, and hands-on experiences that allow each student to grow at their own pace. Whether a child is stepping onto a stage for the first time or refining advanced communication skills, we meet them where they are and guide them forward.

At Lucien Academy, we're not just teaching public speaking, writing, or performance — we're shaping confident communicators, creative thinkers, and future leaders.

"We're not just teaching public speaking, writing, or performance — we're shaping confident communicators, creative thinkers, and future leaders."

What We Teach

Our core programs

Five thoughtfully designed programs that grow with your child — from first-time speakers to advanced young authors and performers.

🎤

Public Speaking

Confidence begins with courage and practice.

Our public speaking program helps children speak up, stand tall, and share their ideas with the world. Students participate in debates, speeches, presentations, storytelling showcases, and mock conferences designed to strengthen real-world communication skills.

  • Build stage confidence
  • Organize & present ideas clearly
  • Improve articulation & projection
  • Develop persuasive speaking
  • Overcome stage fright
  • Debate & impromptu skills
✍️

Creative Writing

Every child has a story to tell.

Our creative writing program nurtures imagination and structured expression. Students develop strong literacy foundations while discovering their unique voice — and can even publish their own story books.

  • Story development & plot building
  • Character creation
  • Poetry & personal narratives
  • Speech writing
  • Script writing
  • Bilingual writing (EN & FR)
🎭

Drama & Performing Arts

Expression builds confidence.

Through drama, children explore emotion, character, and collaboration. Our performing arts program strengthens empathy, confidence, creativity, and communication skills in a fun and supportive environment.

  • Improvisation
  • Character development
  • Scripted performance
  • Emotional expression
  • Team collaboration
  • Stage presence
🇫🇷

French & Bilingual Communication

Language opens doors.

Whether students are in French Immersion or simply eager to expand their language skills, we help them communicate confidently in both official languages.

  • French conversation
  • French public speaking
  • French creative writing
  • Bilingual presentation skills
  • Reading & comprehension
  • Literacy enrichment
💬

Communication & Leadership Skills

We go beyond academics.

Our leadership and communication enrichment programs prepare children not just for school success — but for life success.

  • Debate & critical thinking
  • Interview skills
  • Presentation coaching
  • Collaborative projects
  • Confidence-building workshops
  • Social communication skills

How We Teach

Our approach

At Lucien Academy, we believe learning should be engaging, encouraging, interactive, and empowering. We use small group instruction, experiential learning, performance-based assessments, and individualized feedback to help every child grow at their own pace.

Engaging

Hands-on, experiential lessons that keep every child curious and involved.

Encouraging

Individualized feedback that builds self-belief and celebrates every step forward.

Interactive

Small-group instruction and collaboration so every voice is heard.

Empowering

Performance-based growth that turns students into confident young leaders.
